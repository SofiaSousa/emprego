About Frontkom

Frontkom is a digital consultancy agency based in Norway that specializes in developing and implementing open-source solutions.

Frontkom has an expanding team and is recruiting a Mid/Senior Full Stack Drupal Developer to work at the Funchal office.

The selected candidate will have primary responsibilities to implement web solutions using open source PHP-based web content management systems.

Profile

  • Ease of communication (spoken and written).
  • Ability to interact with customers, technical and non-technical
  • Strong sense of responsibility and teamwork.
  • Good organization and time management skills.
  • Interest in learning new technologies.
  • Availability to travel abroad.

Skills

Requirements

  • Minimum educational qualifications: High-school (12th year).
  • Good knowledge of English (spoken and written).
  • Experience with Drupal CMS, version 8/9.
  • PHP development experience, with the latest features of PHP 7 and MySQL/MariaDB.
  • Development experience with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
  • Knowledge of content management systems (CMS).

Valued skills

  • Higher education in Computer Engineering or equivalent.
  • Experience with WordPress CMS.
  • Experience with CSS preprocessors and front-end frameworks.
  • Knowledge of Apache and Nginx web servers.
  • Knowledge of version control systems.
