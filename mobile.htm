About Frontkom

Frontkom is a digital consultancy agency based in Norway that specializes in developing and implementing open-source solutions.

Frontkom has an expanding team and is recruiting a full-time Junior Mobile Developer to work at the Funchal office, or remotely from Portugal.

Please note that, as this is a Junior entry-level position, no in-depth knowledge of the technologies described below is required. Frontkom always provides time and resources for training during the onboarding of a developer.

The selected candidate will have primary responsibilities to implement mobile native solutions using Android and/or iOS frameworks.

Profile

  • Ease of communication (spoken and written).
  • Ability to interact with customers, technical and non-technical
  • Strong sense of responsibility and teamwork.
  • Good organization and time management skills.
  • Interest in learning new technologies.
  • Availability to travel abroad.

Skills

Requirements

  • Minimum educational qualifications: High-school (12th year).
  • Good knowledge of English (spoken and written).
  • Experience with Android development, using Kotlin and/or Java
  • Experience with iOS development, using Swift

Valued skills

  • Higher education in Computer Engineering or equivalent.
  • Experience with consuming RESTful APIs.
  • Knowledge of version control systems (ex: Git).
  • Knowledge with Project Management platforms, such as Jira.
